We Know Trees.

Planting trees in local communities across the country to support a greener earth.

For every tree you plant with us, we will plant five just like it in your community. Parks, waterfronts, schools, wherever. Trees create the oxygen we use to breathe. We think that's pretty cool. We also think that you should too!

Our Promise to the planet.

World Up is a start-up with a passion for saving our green earth! We plant trees by the thousands to help offset our carbon footprint.
